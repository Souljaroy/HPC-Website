Powering Critical
Infrastructure

Hybrid Power Corp. is a Design-Build Contractor specializing in complete Electro-Mechanical Turnkey Projects. We take full ownership — from engineering design to procurement, installation, testing, and commissioning.

As a licensed supplier and contractor, we deliver HV, MV, and LV electrical systems backed by UL listings and trusted international standards. Our team brings deep expertise across manufacturing plants, industrial facilities, data centers, and commercial buildings.

Turnkey Delivery

End-to-End Project Execution

We handle every phase of your electro-mechanical project — from initial engineering to final handover. No gaps, no subcontractor confusion. One contract, full accountability.

01
Engineering Design
Single-line diagrams, load flow analysis, equipment schedules, and full electrical design documentation compliant with PEC and international standards.
02
Procurement & Supply
Direct supply of UL-listed switchgears, cable trays, busduct systems, transformers, generators, panelboards, and all supporting equipment.
03
Installation & Construction
PCAB-licensed construction team handles all civil, electro-mechanical installation works with strict adherence to safety and quality standards.
04
Testing & Commissioning
Comprehensive factory acceptance testing, site acceptance testing, and full system commissioning with complete documentation and O&M manuals.
05
Handover & After-Sales Support
Project handover with as-built drawings, warranty support, and ongoing maintenance services to keep your systems running at peak performance.
